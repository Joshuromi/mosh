Our Love Story

First Meet

06 December 2020

Monisola and Joshua first met at The Headstone church. Love was the furthest thing from their minds. A shared smile, a kind word, a casual greeting and simple moments that felt ordinary. Little did they know, those brief encounters were the seeds of something extraordinary. What began as a chance meeting in a place of faith slowly blossomed into a bond they never expected, a love guided by grace and destined to flourish.

First Date

25 June 2023

An unplanned spark brought Monisola and Joshua together on their first date. Their first date was simple yet magical, filled with moments that felt both familiar and new. Every glance, every word carried a deeper meaning now. What began as simple companionship took an unexpected turn, as their conversations and laughter revealed a connection they hadn’t seen before — a love story quietly unfolding.

Proposal

18 December 2023

With a heartfelt smile, Joshua spoke of their unexpected journey, how their friendship blossomed into a love he could no longer imagine life without. He asked the question that held the weight of forever and softened with joy, Monisola said yes knowing their love had grown into something truly special. Their love story taking its next, beautiful step.

Enagagement

14 December 2024

14th of December 2024, a moment that will mark the beginning of a new chapter for Monisola and Joshua. Their journey, from unexpected friendship to a deep connection, will be sealed with a promise to walk through life side by side. Surrounded by the warmth of family and friends, this day will be a celebration of the love that has grown between them, not defined by grand gestures, but by genuine connection and shared dreams.
